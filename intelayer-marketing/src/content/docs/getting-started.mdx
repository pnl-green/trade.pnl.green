# Getting Started

Welcome to Intelayer, the crypto-first multi-venue terminal. This guide walks through onboarding, the default workspace, and handy shortcuts so you can ship trades faster.

## Create your Intelayer account

1. Visit [intelayer.com](https://intelayer.com) and launch **Trade Now**.
2. Sign up with email + password or OAuth where available.
3. Confirm your email address to unlock live trading.

## Quick tour

- **Workspace presets**: Choose between Momentum, Market Neutral, and Scalps. Each preset preloads layouts for DOMs, charts, and the AI-CLI overlay.
- **Portfolio panel**: Aggregates balances and PnL across every connected venue.
- **Activity feed**: Tracks fills, alerts, and bot edits in real time.

## Keyboard shortcuts

Intelayer ships with sensible defaults. Press `?` inside the app to open the cheat sheet.

- `⌘/Ctrl + K` – Command palette
- `T` – Toggle AI-CLI overlay
- `⌘/Ctrl + B` – Open bot builder
- `⌘/Ctrl + Shift + M` – Switch workspaces
